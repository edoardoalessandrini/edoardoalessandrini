The morning didn't started in the best way, it was very cold and our clothes were wet so we stayed in our sleeping bag for 2 hours after waking up. We left very late in the morning and started cycling near the lake, it was very very long. It was a little bit difficult, a continuos up and down, natural hills or overpasses, each kilometer first went up and then down. We climbed Col du Perty with a nice view and scent of lavender fields, always cloudly with a storm nearby. We arrived after dinner in our Airbnb. The village was very very small, only few houses, no supermarkets, restaurants or other shops.

  • Ride time: 6h 5min 47sec

Notes

Finally in one of the most famous mountains for cyclists. After a good breakfast we started approaching the climb doing 40km. The number of bikes increased and in fact the climb was very busy, important day in France. Without our bags the bike was very light and the climb easy. Arrived on the top we descent a little bit to find a good spot and wait the Tour. It was a little bit windy and not so hot, the lunch very poor, with only an energetic bar and a banana. Francesco realized his tire was broken and during the descent the inner tube exploded. After fixing it with scotch we urgently looked for a bike shop and bought a new tire before it closed.

Notes

We paid 1 euro for 1h30min train from Montpellier to Perpignan, bike transport free. We couldn't not do it. After asking Strava to two girls we met on the train who never accepted our request we started moving very excited for the entrance in Spain, our first crossing of a country that does not border Italy. After the photo at the sign we quickly went down to the first city (Figueres) between trucks. while we were standing on a sidewalk a man came out of a pizzeria thinking we had punctured the wheel. We discovered that he was Italian, Sardinian. He let us in and we ate a good pizza made by a man from Naples. Best way to enter in Spain. Then we continued to Girona where we slept in the hostel and ate in the best Indian restaurant ever tasted.

Tibidabo is the "Spanish Superga", the famous church in Turin.
