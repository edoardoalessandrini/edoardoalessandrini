Notes

Milan is 140/150 km from Turin. Covering the distance by bike is very flat but not so short, maybe boring, for these reason I’ve always skipped this tour. Today there’s the Policrit, a criterium race where the bike team I’m associated with participates. I will not do the race, but I think it’s a good excuse to ride on the flat route of the Pianura Padana. After an important carbo load I started in Z2 with my friend Francesco. The pace wasn't quick, we chatted while riding very chill. After 5 hours, some food and a pizza in a kebab shop, we arrived. Our stop was very short: we met some friends, but Francesco had to return by train early, so I decided to ride back by bike. The return was a good moment to train my mind — it was the first time I did 140 km alone, with only two short bathroom breaks. I arrived at sunset and I was very happy with how I handled the food, the effort, and the mental side.
