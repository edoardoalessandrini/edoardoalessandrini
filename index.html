About me

Hi, I’m Edoardo. I was born in 2002 in Turin, Italy, where I still live. I take a multidisciplinary approach to life, learning from each experience and applying it to the next, always looking for new goals and working to turn my ideas into reality. I hold a degree in ICT and a master’s degree in Data Analysis, but I also learn a lot from sports, which have been part of my life since childhood. From mountains to the sea, I do many activities, and now I’m especially focused on cycling — and on eating enough to burn the calories.
